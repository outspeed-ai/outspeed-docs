title: "Gemini Multimodal Live"
description: "Google's multimodal live streaming model (Coming Soon)"
---

Gemini Multimodal Live is Google's model for real-time multimodal interactions. It will support:

- Real-time video understanding
- Live speech conversation
- Multimodal streaming
- Voice customization

This model will be supported by [speech console](/get-started/speech-console) soon.

For more information, visit [Google's Gemini documentation](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/docs/multimodal-live).
